Welcome to Salon App

This Application has been developed to bring daily salon services near customers as they can ease their life by simply booking with different salons of thier priority

This Salon App will help customers get services from their priority salons at reduced costs and at the time of convinience. The customers will be able to see details of available salons and the different services offered on this platform. Customers will only be required to make a booking at a desired time at any Salon available and of choice.

Problem being solved

User Challenges

Very many students around campus find it quite hard to locate salons that offer a variety of services say massages, hair trimming, and hair plaiting. This App is meant to help customers find nearby salons that offer a variety of services and extreme convenience. Salons that have been included in this App offer the above mentioned services at a quite reduced expenses.
